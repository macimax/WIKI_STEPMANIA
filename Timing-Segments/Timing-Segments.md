A **timing segment** is an element of a simfile that can affect gameplay. There are multiple types:

* [BPM](bpm-segment)
* [Stop](stop-segment)
* [Delay](delay-segment)
* [TimeSignature](time-signature-segment)
* [Warp](warp-segment)
* [Combo](combo-segment)
* [Tickcount](tickcount-segment)
* [Label](label-segment)
* [Speed](speed-segment)
* [Scroll](scroll-segment)
* [Fake](fake-segment)
