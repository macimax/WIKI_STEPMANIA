A **timing segment** is an element of a simfile that can affect gameplay. There are multiple types:

* [BPM](timing-segments/bpm)
* [Stop](timing-segments/stop)
* [Delay](timing-segments/delay)
* [TimeSignature](timing-segments/time-signature)
* [Warp](timing-segments/warp)
* [Combo](timing-segments/combo)
* [Tickcount](timing-segments/tickcount)
* [Label](timing-segments/label)
* [Speed](timing-segments/speed)
* [Scroll](timing-segments/scroll)
* [Fake](timing-segments/fake)
